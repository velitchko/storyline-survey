Description of layout algorithm: Storylines with a character being in all meetings (protagonist). One-sided and two-sided storylines are considered: All characters are on one side of the protagonist or on both sides (but they must stay on that side). One-sided crossing minimization is shown to be poly, two-sided NP-hard. One- and two-sided block crossing minimization is shown to be NP-hard.
