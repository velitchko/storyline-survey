Description of layout algorithm: MILP for computing the complete layout. Characters are placed at discrete coordinates with equal spacing. Standard ILP for crossing minimization. Wiggle and Skewness (number of characters to remove to be planar) are also optimized. All these criteria have adjustable parameters with which they can be weighted in the objective. Further, also fairness of wiggle, skewness, and crossings can be optimized, weighted with further parameters. A post-processing wiggle minimization is also proposed when the ILP does not optimize wiggle. In that case, characters do not have equal spacing. Iteratively and greedily groups of characters are moved up or down as long as wiggle is reduced
