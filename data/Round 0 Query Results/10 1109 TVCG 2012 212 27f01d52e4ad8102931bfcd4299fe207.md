Description of layout algorithm: Minimization of wiggle, crossings, and whitespace by genetic algorithm with postprocessing. Interactions can be multiple timesteps long. A genome assigns each interaction an integer $y$-slot. To evaluate a genome, a pipeline approach of rearranging lines inside slots and removing whitespace is applied and then a fitness function is employed that measures wiggle and crossings. To rearrange lines inside a slot, lines are categorized and greedily made as straight as possible. Minimizing whitespace greedily shifts slot segments (Connected clusters inside a slot) down. An adaptation incorporating locations is presented, where a fixed order of locations is also encoded with a genome.
